Guide to F1

if you're new to F1, this is the perfect time to join the fun

Grand Prix

Grand Prix is easily the most important and most exciting part of F1 as it is most anticipated. Grand Prix takes place over 3 days, from Friday to Sunday. This is known as Race Weekend where drivers get two 60 minute Free Practice Sessions on Friday(FP1 and FP2). On Saturday, drivers are given another practice session in the morning(FP3).

On Saturday afternoon, an hour of qualifying is split into three Sessions which forms the starting grid for the Grand Prix. The race is the main event of the weekned and its where the points are delivered to the teams and drivers. The race features elements of stategy, with pit stops and fuel managemtn to consider.

Qualifying session

Qualifying sessions determine the race order and the session is divided into three sects : Q1, Q2, and Q3. Q1 lasts 18 minutes, with 20 drivers and knocks out five of the slowest drivers which occupy seats 16-20, with 2o being final position. Q2 lasts 15 minutes and knocks out another 5 of the slowest drivers and they occupy grid spaces 11-15. Q3 lasts 12 minutes with 10 drivers which determines starting position of grid.

Drivers Standings

During the race season, each driver out of 20 has a chance to earn points in each race and win the World Drivers Championship. Points are awarded where the driver places. 1st place is awarded 25 points, 2nd place is awarded 18 points, 3rd place gets 15 points, 4th place is awarded 12 points, 5th place is 10 points, 6th gets 8 points, 7th place is awarded 6 points, 8th place is awarded 4 points, 9th place is 2 points, 10th place is 1 point and 11-20 earn 0 points.

Constructors Standings

Each team in F1 has a pair of drivers that competes against other pair of drivers to help the team to earn the title of 'best team in F1'. Each driver of a team will individually earn points so that they collectively earn points for their team. Over the recent years, Mercedes AMG Petronas Motorsport has won the 8 Constructors Standings over the last 8 seasons.
