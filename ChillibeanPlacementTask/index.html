What is F1?

Formula One is the highest level of racing in motorsport, where each team, with their drivers and mechanics racing against each other to see who claims the best in the world. The name refers to the rule established by the International Automobile Federation, which all participants and cars must comply with. The organisation amends alters and introduces rules to Formula One so as to ensure the safety of the participants in the races, promote fair play, and ensure that the followers and spectators of Formula One get a good deal. Formula One seasons consist of 23 races where 10 teams compete towards the Constructors World Championship to see who is the best F1 team in the world. Each team is assigned 2 drivers and each driver competes with others for the World Drivers Championship. F1 is considered one of the most exciting sports with F1 car's average speed of 260kph making F1 fun to watch.

Why I love Formula One

Formula One is an amazing sport which involves intense races, high-speed cars and close battles. I love watching the races to see which driver can come out on top as each race is unpredictable. Aside from watching the races, I enjoy playing formula one video games in my spare time as it allows me to simulate what a F1 race is really like. BTW Mercedes is the best team on the gird!

Notable F1 Drivers

Most famous drivers who have won many races and have made the sport more exciting.

Lewis Hamilton

7 time World Champion who is widely regarded one of the greatest F1 has ever seen and has done unimaginable things for the sport.

Max Verstappen

The youngest driver to win a race in F1 was crowned World Champion in 2021 and he is one of the most talented drivers on the grid.

Sebastian Vettel

The 4 time World Champion is the youngest driver to win a World Champion having won 4 in a row from 2010-2013.
